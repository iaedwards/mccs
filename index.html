Growing. Challenging. Transforming.

Mt. Carmel Christian School is a place where children from all walks of life can come and thrive. Our small class sizes and the opportunity for extensive individualized attention enables our students to feel safe, special, and challenged to do their very best. We further believe that students can be taught to love learning, and that if the level of expectation is raised, the students will rise to the occasion.

Through a rigorous academic curriculum high expectations for behavior, the arts and athletics, Mt. Carmel endeavors to instill in young people a desire for a relationship with Jesus Christ; a love of country and consciousness of citizenship; habits of industry and thoroughness; and a life-long love of learning.
