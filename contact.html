Directions

From the Greensburg Area and Locations North.

Follow Route 119 South and pass the Scottdale (Route 819) Exit. At the second traffic light past the exit, turn left at George's Trading Post. At the stop sign, turn left onto Mt. Pleasant Road. Proceed approximately one mile until you see the tan school building and the Mount Carmel Community Church sign on your left. Turn left into the school drive between the school and church. The church is now on your right and school is on your left. Park behind the church building. The school entrance is marked "Pritts Hall."

From the Uniontown Area and Locations South.

Follow Route 119 North and pass the Everson exit. Turn right at the next traffic light at George's Trading Post. At the stop sign, turn left onto Mount Pleasant Road. Proceed approximately one mile until you see the tan school building and the Mount Carmel Community Church sign on your left. Turn left into the school drive between the school and church. The church is now on your right and school is on your left. Park behind the church building. The school entrance is marked "Pritts Hall."
