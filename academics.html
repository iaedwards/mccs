Kindergarten · Elementary

The younger Cougars take the first steps of their thirteen-year journey. Curricula for the different grades includes a heavy emphasis in forming a thorough knowledge base in the core areas of reading, math, science, and Bible. Check out this page for more important information and forms.

Junior High

Having passed the half-way point, this group of Cougars adds new levels of responsibility and opportunity at Mount Carmel including formalized athletic competition and participation in the annual Veterans Day Program.

Senior High

In their final years at Mount Carmel, Cougars navigate their most difficult and yet rewarding challenges ever. Efforts in Physics, Calculus, and Shakespeare as well as Varsity Competition are compensated by heightened readiness for the upcoming collegiate years.
