Box Tops for Education

Box Tops for Education is an easy way for our school to earn up to $20,000 each year! You can clip Box Tops from hundreds of your favorite products; Shop online at over 200 stores throughout the Box Tops Marketplace; or Charge with the Box Tops for Education Visa card. You can also sign up for the Box Tops Booster Club where you can see our school's earnings; Get more chances to win Bonus Box Tops; Print money-saving coupons; Shop online and earn cash for our school; or Stay up-to-date with Box Tops newsletters.

There are three easy steps to earning cash with Box Tops:

  1. Clip Box Tops from participating products.
  2. Send the Box Tops in to school.
  3. Earn cash for our school - 10 cents for each Box Top!
Please visit the BoxTops4Education.com website to learn more about how to help our school earn cash through this wonderful program. Thanks for all of your help!

Labels for Education

The Labels for Education program is a great way to get FREE merchandise for our school, and Campbell's makes it so easy to redeem labels. Participating in the program is easy - simply save labels from Campbell products and send them to school. To see the complete list of products that are eligible for the program, please click here to go to the Campbell's Labels for Education website. Triple your efforts by asking your friends and family to collect labels on behalf of our school. Community members can pledge their support by making a Community Label Pledge at labelsforeducation.com. Simply invite friends and family to visit the website, and click into the "Community Label Pledge" link. Here they can make a voluntary pledge to donate Campbell product labels to our school. Thanks so much for your support. Together with Campbell's Labels for Education - we can build a better school for our children - one label at a time.
