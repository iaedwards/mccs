Box Tops for Education

Box Tops for Education is an easy way for our school to earn up to $20,000 each year! You can clip Box Tops from hundreds of your favorite products, shop on-line at over 200 stores in the Box Tops Marketplace, or charge with the Box Tops for Education Visa card.

You can also sign up for the Box Tops Booster Club to see our school's earnings, get more chances to win bonus Box Tops, print money-saving coupons, or shop on-line to earn cash for our school.

There are three easy steps to earning cash with Box Tops:

  1. Clip Box Tops from participating products.
  2. Send the Box Tops in to school.
  3. Earn cash for our school - 10 cents for each Box Top!
Visit BoxTops4Education.com to learn more.

Labels for Education

The Labels for Education program is a great way to get FREE merchandise for our school, and Campbell's makes it easy to redeem labels. Simply save labels from Campbell products and send them to school. To see the complete list of products that are eligible for the program, please go to the Campbell's Labels for Education website. Triple your efforts by asking your friends and family to collect labels on behalf of our school. Community members can pledge their support by making a Community Label Pledge at labelsforeducation.com. Simply invite friends and family to visit the website and click into the "Community Label Pledge" link. Here they can make a voluntary pledge to donate Campbell product labels to our school.

Thanks so much for your support.
