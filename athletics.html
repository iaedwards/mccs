Welcome to the Homepage of the Mt. Carmel Cougars!

Mt. Carmel has an athletic offering that includes fall and and winter Men’s Soccer and Basketball as well as Women’s Volleyball and Basketball. Additionally, the Cougars train for an annual all-day Track & Field meet for both boys and girls.

Visit the individual team pages for schedules, scores, and photos from each of the teams.. Our athletic program strives to develop healthy students and cultivate a sportsman-like, Christian attitude in competition. We endeavor to prepare our young people physically and mentally to thoroughly know and play the game to the best of their God-given ability.
